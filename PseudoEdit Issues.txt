ISSUES

Issue #1:
If the caret is at the end of a line and you press right arrow, it skips into the next line.
The activeLine is then not updated in the KeyDown Event, as it's only looking for ArrowUp and ArrowDown.
It's not that important, as the activeLine is then updated in the KeyUp event.
The same problem occurs if the caret is at the start of a line and you press left arrow.

Importance: Minor Annoyance


IDEAS

Idea #1:
When you click on a line number, that whole line should be selected automatically.
